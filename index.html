About This Project

This crowdfunding initiative is dedicated to supporting survivors of Gender-Based Violence (GBV) and funding programs that provide shelter, counseling, legal aid, and education. By backing this project, you contribute to a safer society where victims receive the necessary care and support to rebuild their lives.
